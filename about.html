Background

An Organisation that has Had a Humble Begininning dating back to the late 60's with the initiative of Late John Patrick Obwapus. This Organisation has evolved from Katamarwa-mboira Farm Located at Katamarwa-Mboira, Kigumba Mboira Subcounties, Kiryandongo District,Uganda.
Its Located 20Kms, a 20 minutes drive from a major Economic Centre (Masindi), It Ventures in Mixed Farming practicing Crop and Animal Husbandry. The Sole Aim was to establish a modern model commercial entity which is to act as a centre of research and therefore a service of information, funds, materials and other Technologies to the population with in and out of the locality henceforth boosting local and National economic development. The Farming Initiative can access atleast upto 500 hectares of land
