Katamarwa Mboira Agri Initiatives LTD- Vision

Advocate for elimination of Illiteracy and Eradication of poverty through Mobilisation,Emanicipation and Empowerment of her members for Economic, Political and Social Development.

Goals And Objectives

1. To Grow the farm into a research/ demonstration unit where peasants/ farmers will be able to lean from

2. Improve on Community health by providing/supplying dietry foods such as a variety of fruits, other crop and animal products

3. Increase on the community income through daily income generating activity or projects

4. Create a food and income busket especially for people affected and infected by HIV/AIDs scourge.

Transformation

Transform people of Katamarwa Mboira and Neighbouring areas from peasantry life to a modern community with high levels of literacy and urbanisation.

Seek Justice

Through increased incomes, members are able to seek justice both from the community they live in and also from government.

Empower Members

With increased Agricultural Economic activities and Education, members are empowered with knowledge and literacy to become better and urban financially sound communities.
